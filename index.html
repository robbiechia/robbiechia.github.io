About Me

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Robbie, a graduating Data Science & Analytics student from NUS. I thoroughly enjoy the process of breaking down problem statements and solving them, which are easily fulfilled by the field of data analysis and applied data science through machine learning, statistics and AI. I care a lot about not just building things that work, but understanding why they work (or don't).

I tend to approach problems by asking a lot of questions before writing any code. In my experience, the first version of the problem is almost never the actual problem — and digging into the data usually reveals something more interesting.

Outside of Data Science & Analytics

I'm a people-oriented person who loves connecting with others. I enjoy meeting new people, learning fresh perspectives and diving into interesting conversations about anything under the sun. I enjoy playing sports like tchoukball, and enjoying music through playing it on my guitar.

What Gets Me Going

I'm drawn to problems that are complex and ambiguous, where the path to a solution isn't clear. I find it rewarding to navigate through that uncertainty and arrive at insights that can make a real difference especially through working with others too. I also love the process of learning itself — there's something deeply satisfying about mastering a new technique, understanding a new concept, or discovering a new perspective.
